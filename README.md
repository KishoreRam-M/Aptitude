### What You MUST Learn First (Days 1-3)

**Basic Algebra**
- Solve: x + 5 = 10 ‚Üí x = 5
- Why: Ages, work, mixture problems use equations.

**Tables & Squares**
- Memorize: 1-20 multiplication tables
- Memorize: Squares like 15¬≤ = 225, 12¬≤ = 144
- Why: Speed. Saves 5 minutes per test.

**Mental Math Tricks**

- Why: Tests are timed. Mental math builds confidence.

## üìÖ PART 4: 15-DAY STUDY PLAN

### DAY 1: NUMBER BASICS
- **Learn**: Add, subtract, multiply, divide (0-1000)
- **Time**: 2 hours
- **Practice**: 50 questions (all basic arithmetic)
- **Goal**: 95% accuracy without calculator

### DAY 2: FRACTIONS & DECIMALS
- **Learn**: 1/2 = 0.5 = 50%; convert between forms
- **Time**: 2.5 hours
- **Practice**: Add fractions (1/2 + 1/3), decimals
- **Goal**: Fluent conversions

### DAY 3: PERCENTAGES & ALGEBRA
- **Learn**: 25% of 200 = 50; solve x + 3 = 10
- **Time**: 2.5 hours
- **Practice**: % calculations, 30 simple equations
- **Goal**: Solve 20 equations mentally

### DAY 4: TABLES, SQUARES, MENTAL MATH
- **Learn**: Memorize tables 1-20; squares (10¬≤ to 20¬≤)
- **Time**: 2 hours
- **Practice**: Quick mental calculations
- **Goal**: Instant recall (no thinking time)

### DAY 5: RATIO & PROPORTION
- **Learn**: 2:3 means "2 parts vs 3 parts"; 5 parts total
- **Time**: 3 hours
- **Practice**: 40 easy ratio questions
- **Goal**: Divide anything into ratios

### DAY 6: AVERAGE
- **Learn**: Average = (sum of numbers) / (count)
- **Time**: 2.5 hours
- **Practice**: 30 average questions; weighted average intro
- **Goal**: Solve without formula confusion

### DAY 7: HCF & LCM
- **Learn**: HCF = greatest common divisor; LCM = least common multiple
- **Time**: 2.5 hours
- **Practice**: Find HCF(12,18) = 6; LCM(4,6) = 12
- **Goal**: Use factorization method smoothly

### DAY 8: AGES
- **Learn**: "A is 2√ó B; in 5 years, A = 20"
- **Time**: 3 hours
- **Practice**: 25 age problems (easy only)
- **Goal**: Solve with equations step-by-step

### DAY 9: TIME & WORK
- **Learn**: A completes in 5 days = 1/5 work per day
- **Time**: 3 hours
- **Practice**: 30 work questions
- **Goal**: Understand efficiency = 1/time

### DAY 10: PIPES & CISTERN
- **Learn**: Same as work (Pipe A fills, Pipe B drains)
- **Time**: 2.5 hours
- **Practice**: 25 pipe questions
- **Goal**: Apply work logic to filling

### DAY 11: TIME & DISTANCE
- **Learn**: Speed = distance / time; units matter
- **Time**: 2.5 hours
- **Practice**: 30 speed/distance questions
- **Goal**: Keep units consistent (km/hr)

### DAY 12: TRAINS & HEIGHT
- **Learn**: Two trains crossing; ladder on wall angles
- **Time**: 3 hours
- **Practice**: 20 train + 15 height questions
- **Goal**: Relative speed concepts

### DAY 13: PROFIT & LOSS
- **Learn**: CP = cost; SP = selling price; Profit% = (SP-CP)/CP √ó 100
- **Time**: 3 hours
- **Practice**: 40 profit/loss + discount questions
- **Goal**: % on correct base (CP, not SP)

### DAY 14: SIMPLE & COMPOUND INTEREST
- **Learn**: SI = P √ó R √ó T / 100; CI = P(1 + R/100)^T
- **Time**: 3 hours
- **Practice**: 35 interest questions (SI then CI)
- **Goal**: Compare SI vs CI confidently

### DAY 15: PERMUTATIONS, PROBABILITY & REVIEW
- **Learn**: Permutations (arrange 3 books); Probability (coin flip)
- **Time**: 4 hours
- **Practice**: 40 perm + 35 probability + 30 mixed review
- **Goal**: Full test 30 mixed questions at 70%+

## üí™ PART 5: HOW TO STUDY (NOT BORING)

### Daily Routine
```
25 min study + 5 min break (Pomodoro timer)
Do this 4 times = 2 hours
Best time: Morning (brain fresh for math)
```

### The 3-Step Learning Process
1. **Watch Video** (10 min from Khan Academy / Unacademy)
2. **Copy Formula & Examples** (5 min; understand, not memorize)
3. **Solve Problems** (45 min; easy first, then moderate)

### Questions Per Day
- **Easy**: 20-30 questions (build confidence)
- **Moderate**: 15-25 questions (check understanding)
- **Hard**: 5-10 questions (optional, tackle later)

### Handling Mistakes
- **When you get it wrong**: 
  - Don't feel bad‚Äîmark it ‚ùå
  - Redo the same question 3 times next day
  - Ask "Why wrong?" (forgot formula? units? reading error?)
- **Mistakes are teachers**‚Äîbest way to learn

### Build Confidence
- **End every day with 5 wins** (easiest questions)
- **Track success**: Keep a notebook: "Today: 45/50 = 90%! üéâ"
- **Use a calendar**: X mark each day you study (don't break the chain)
- **Celebrate small wins**: Solved ratios? That's progress!

### If You Get Stuck
- **Don't give up**
- **Watch 2-min explanation video** (hint, not answer)
- **Sleep on it**, try again tomorrow
- **10-min rule**: If stuck >10 min, ask for hint (not solution)

### Weekly Review
- **Every morning (10 min)**: Redo yesterday's 10 wrong questions
- **Every Saturday (1 hour)**: Full practice test from that week

## ‚ö†Ô∏è PART 6: COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### Calculation Mistakes
- **Error**: 17 √ó 6 = 92 (wrong!)
- **Fix**: Always estimate: 17 ‚âà 20 ‚Üí 20 √ó 6 = 120, so answer near 100 ‚úì

### Ratio Mistakes
- **Error**: Divide by 2 instead of 5 parts in 2:3 ratio
- **Fix**: Draw lines: ||| || = 5 parts total. Then 2/5 and 3/5.

### Work & Pipes Mistakes
- **Error**: Forget to invert; A does in 4 days but use 4 not 1/4
- **Fix**: Rate = 1/time always. A = 1/4 per day.

### Speed & Distance Mistakes
- **Error**: Mix units (km/hr vs m/s)
- **Fix**: Write units every time. 60 km/hr, not just 60.

### Profit & Interest Mistakes
- **Error**: Profit% on SP instead of CP
- **Fix**: Profit% = (SP - CP) / **CP** √ó 100. Always CP.

### Permutation & Probability Mistakes
- **Error**: Forget "with replacement" cases
- **Fix**: List small cases first (coin toss, cards)

### All Topics
- **Error**: Skip steps, write nothing, panic
- **Fix**: Write every formula, underline answer, no shortcuts
